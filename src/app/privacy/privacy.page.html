  • 🔐 All user data is transmitted through encrypted communication protocols to ensure security.
  • 🛡️ The system uses a highly secure database with strictly controlled access—only authorized users can view or modify the data.

When using the system, we expect users to follow these security guidelines:
  • 🔑 Keep your account and password safe, and do not share them with unauthorized individuals.
